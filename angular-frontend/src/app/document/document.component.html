Telecommunication Network

In less than a century, the means of telecommunication have greatly diversified. Depending on the type of information transmitted, one can distinguish between sound (telephone, sound broadcasting), image (videography), sound and image (television), text (telegraph, telex, fax, teletext), computer data(telecomputing), etc. According to the mode of provision of the users, one can distinguish the means of telecommunication always functioning in one direction, from a transmitter towards a receiver (broadcasting, television, paging…), and those which make it possible to establish a dialogue between two people or two groups (telephone) or between, on one side, a person or a group and, on the other, a machine offering a battery of services (videotex). echnology continues to reshape businesses and even the way we communicate with our peers on a daily basis.
